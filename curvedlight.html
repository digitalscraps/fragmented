Curved Light

Curved Light is the emerging solo project of hardware synth composer Peter Tran, who makes his Holodeck Records debut with the new mixtape Channelview. In addition to his natural flair for experimentation, Peter truly shines at crafting pieces with sophistication beyond the standard practice of modular synthesizers.
